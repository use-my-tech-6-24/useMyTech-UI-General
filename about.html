Our Mission

Some of us want to use high-quality tech equipment for special events, without making the investment or paying high fees to big rental companies. We get it. Use My Tech Stuff aims to provide a safe, guaranteed, and efficient way to effortlessly share professional tech equipment. Don’t settle for using a point-and-shoot for that special trip, but don’t pay too much for something better. Choose from hundreds of camera, computer, speaker, and lighting models that you won’t find anywhere else.
